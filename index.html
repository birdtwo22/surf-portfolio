About

Water's never been the scary part.

I took swim lessons at 11 and have kept at it since — including a 2024–2026 stretch of adult morning classes, three times a week before work. That's given me an easy confidence around water that most beginners on a surf shop floor don't have, and it's part of why I understand what a nervous first-timer needs to hear.

I picked up surfing this year and I'm hooked — balance is still a work in progress, but I'm putting in the hours because I genuinely want this, not just for the job. Long term, I'd love to get certified and teach.

Outside the water, I practice yoga, which keeps my balance and body awareness sharp — the same instincts that carry over onto a board.
